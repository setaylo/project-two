About Game

Dawn has some cleaning up to do, but four chaotic forces of nature stand in her way. Meet the girls one by one and learn why they are no longer on speaking terms. Resolving tensions will take all of the patience and empathy Dawn is able to muster, but with your puzzle-solving and clue-hunting skills, you might just get through it all without anyone shooting the messenger.

FAQ

Where can I download your game?

All upcoming playable demos and game versions will be available to download on our website. Check back right here for updates on where else we might be in the future!

What programs were used to make the game?

Adobe Photoshop for the pixel art and RPGMaker MV to code it all together.

Where do you guys get your ideas?

From out of our heads. Look, there’s no good snappy way to answer this question. Just try making stuff until it’s something you like and show it to people you trust. Wear your inspirations on your sleeve. And don’t take orders from a website!
